# How to make fireworks

A **Firework Rocket** is a little item you can set off to make a big burst of
bright sparks fly up into the air. It's a fun way to celebrate finishing a build!

In CavEX you **craft** the firework yourself, then hold it and press the use
button to make it pop. Here's how.

![A firework burst in CavEX](https://github.com/amahpour/CavEX/releases/download/verification/issue-31-01-firework.png)

## What you need

To craft **one** Firework Rocket you need just two things:

* **1 Paper**
* **1 Gunpowder**

## How to craft it

The firework recipe is a tall shape — **two squares, one on top of the other**:

```
┌───────────┐
│   Paper   │   ← top
├───────────┤
│ Gunpowder │   ← directly below
└───────────┘
```

1. Put the **Paper** in a crafting square.
2. Put the **Gunpowder** in the square **directly below** the paper.
3. Out pops **1 Firework Rocket**! 🎆

**Order matters!** The paper has to be on **top** and the gunpowder on the
**bottom**. If you flip them (gunpowder on top, paper underneath) the recipe will
**not** work.

## How to set it off

First, put the Firework Rocket in your hand by selecting its hotbar slot (the row
of boxes along the bottom of the screen):

* **On a PC:** scroll the mouse wheel until the firework's slot is highlighted.
* **On the Wii:** tap **+** (plus) and **−** (minus) to move along the hotbar
  until the firework's slot is highlighted. See
  [`../../controls.md`](../../controls.md) for the full button list.

Then aim at a **block or the ground** in front of you and press the **use /
place** button:

* **On a PC:** the **right mouse button** (right-click).
* **On the Wii:** the **place** button — the **2** button on the Wiimote, **C**
  on the Nunchuk, or **B** on the Classic Controller. See
  [`../../controls.md`](../../controls.md) for the full button list.

## What happens

A burst of bright sparks shoots **up and outward** from right where you're
standing — a little firework show! ✨

Each time you set one off it uses up **one** firework from your stack, so craft a
whole bunch if you want to keep the party going.

## Quick tips

* **Aim at a block or the ground** when you press the use button — that's where
  the sparks appear. (Pointing straight at the open sky won't set it off.)
* Fireworks stack, so you can carry up to **64** at once and pop them one after
  another.
* Need more? Just craft again — **1 Paper + 1 Gunpowder** makes another rocket
  every time.

Have fun lighting up the sky! 🎆🎇
