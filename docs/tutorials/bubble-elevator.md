# How to make a bubble elevator

A **bubble elevator** is a stack of bubble blocks that lifts you straight up,
like a magic ladder you don't even have to hold onto. Stand inside it and you
float up. Step out and you stop. Easy!

In CavEX you do **not** need any water to make one — the bubble blocks do all
the work by themselves.

## What you need

* **Bubble Column** blocks. This is block **id 98**.
* In a brand-new world they are already waiting for you in **hotbar slot 6**
  (the sixth box along the bottom of the screen). You start with a full stack,
  so there is plenty to build with.

## How to pick the bubbles

First, put the Bubble Column in your hand by selecting hotbar slot 6:

* **On a PC:** scroll the mouse wheel until the sixth slot is highlighted (or
  press the number/scroll keys for the hotbar).
* **On the Wii:** tap the hotbar buttons to move along until the sixth slot is
  highlighted — **+** (plus) moves right and **−** (minus) moves left. See
  [`../../controls.md`](../../controls.md) for the full button list.

## How to build the elevator

1. Look at the floor where you want your elevator to start.
2. Place one Bubble Column block down (right mouse button on PC, or the place
   button on the Wii).
3. Now place another bubble block right **on top** of it.
4. Keep stacking them straight up — one on top of the next — until your tower of
   bubbles is as tall as you want to travel. Each block is one step higher.

That's the whole elevator! A single column of bubble blocks reaching up toward
where you want to go.

## How to ride it

1. Walk into the bottom of the bubble tower so you are **standing inside** the
   bubbles. The blocks are see-through and you can walk right through them — they
   won't block you.
2. You will start floating **upward** all by yourself. Just stay inside the
   column and enjoy the ride up. 🫧
3. When you reach the top — or any floor along the way — **step out to the side**
   onto solid ground. The moment you leave the bubbles, you stop rising.

### Controls while you ride

* **PC:** move with **WASD** and look around with the **mouse**.
* **Wii:** move and look using the controls in
  [`../../controls.md`](../../controls.md).

## Going back down

Bubble elevators in CavEX are **up-only** — they can only lift you, not lower
you. To come back down, just **step out of the column** and walk or jump down
the normal way, the same as stepping off any ledge. (There is no "down" bubble
in this version of the game.)

## Quick tips

* The bubble blocks are **passable** — you can always walk into and out of them,
  so you can never get stuck.
* You float up the **whole time you are inside**, so build your tower as tall as
  the place you want to reach.
* No water, no buttons, no redstone — the bubbles are all you need.

Have fun zooming up to your treehouses and towers! 🏗️
